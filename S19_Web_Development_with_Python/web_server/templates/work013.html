013 : Coffee Shop Dashboard

Interactive dashboard analyzing coffee shop sales data to identify trends, peak hours, and product performance across three NYC locations

View on GitHub
Excel PivotTables PivotCharts Slicers

Monthly revenue trends and daily transaction patterns by hour

Transaction patterns by hour of day across three NYC locations

Project Overview

This project involved creating a comprehensive sales dashboard for Maven Roasters, a coffee shop franchise with three locations in New York City. The dashboard transforms raw transactional data from January to June 2023 into actionable insights for franchise owners to optimize operations and understand customer behavior.

The interactive dashboard enables stakeholders to analyze sales trends, identify peak business hours, understand product performance, and make data-driven decisions to improve profitability.

Business Context

As a new franchise owner at Maven Roasters, I needed to understand purchase behavior across three NYC locations to streamline operations and identify growth opportunities. The raw transactional data required transformation into an intuitive dashboard that could answer key business questions about sales patterns and customer preferences.

Primary Question: How can we transform raw coffee shop transaction data into an interactive dashboard that reveals sales trends, peak business periods, and product performance to drive operational improvements?

Methodology & Analysis

1 Data Preparation & Profiling

  • Explored the dataset to understand transaction volume and time period coverage
  • Added calculated Revenue column (price × quantity)
  • Created Month and Day of Week columns with text formatting ("Jan", "Feb", "Sun", "Mon")
  • Extracted Hour from transaction time for time-based analysis
  • Conducted data quality assessment and validation
  • Profiled product categories and transaction patterns

2 PivotTable Analysis

  • Created revenue by month analysis to identify sales trends
  • Built transaction frequency analysis by day of week and hour of day
  • Analyzed transaction volume by product category
  • Developed Top 15 products analysis by transactions and revenue
  • Organized all PivotTables on a single analysis sheet for dashboard integration

3 Dashboard Development

  • Designed PivotCharts including line charts for time series and column/bar charts for categorical data
  • Implemented store location slicer for interactive filtering across all visualizations
  • Created professional layout with proper alignment and formatting
  • Hidden raw PivotTables and removed gridlines for clean presentation
  • Optimized dashboard for stakeholder usability and insight generation

Key Findings & Insights

Sales Trends Analysis

Metric Finding Business Implication
Peak Sales Days Highest transactions on Wednesdays and Fridays Schedule additional staff and promotions on peak days
Busiest Hours Morning rush (7-9 AM) and lunch hours (12-2 PM) Optimize staffing and inventory management during peak hours
Revenue Trends Consistent growth from January through June Positive business trajectory with seasonal variations
Product Performance Brewed coffee and tea drive highest transaction volume Focus on core product quality and availability

Location-Based Insights

  • Store Variations: Different peak hours and product preferences across locations
  • Revenue Distribution: Balanced sales across all three NYC locations
  • Product Mix: Location-specific preferences requiring tailored inventory
  • Seasonal Patterns: Consistent trends across locations with minor variations

Technical Implementation

Technologies Used

Excel PivotTables PivotCharts Slicers

Analytical Techniques

  • Date and time parsing using Excel functions
  • Revenue calculation and data validation
  • Categorical data formatting for improved readability
  • Dynamic filtering using slicers connected to multiple PivotTables
  • Professional chart formatting and dashboard design principles
  • Data organization for optimal performance and usability

Business Impact

The interactive dashboard enabled Maven Roasters franchise owners to:

  • Optimize staffing schedules based on peak transaction hours
  • Adjust inventory management for high-demand products
  • Develop targeted promotions for underperforming menu items
  • Identify location-specific opportunities for growth
  • Track sales performance and seasonal trends
  • Enhance customer experience during peak hours
Recommendation: Implement targeted promotions during slower business hours to increase off-peak revenue, optimize product mix at each location based on local preferences, and schedule staff training during identified slow periods to maximize productivity. Develop location-specific marketing strategies based on customer behavior patterns and expand data collection to include customer demographics for deeper insights.
