Project Overview

This project involved analyzing transactional data from Maven Roasters, a coffee shop chain with three locations in New York City. The analysis transformed raw sales data into an interactive Excel dashboard to identify purchasing patterns, peak business hours, and product performance trends.

The project demonstrates my expertise in Excel data analysis, pivot table creation, and interactive dashboard design for retail business intelligence.

Business Context

As a franchise owner of Maven Roasters, I needed to understand customer purchasing behavior across three NYC locations to optimize operations, inventory management, and staffing. The dashboard provides actionable insights to improve business performance and customer experience.

Primary Question: How do sales trend over time, which days and hours are busiest, and which products drive the most revenue for Maven Roasters coffee shops?

Methodology & Analysis

1 Data Preparation & Transformation

  • Explored the dataset to understand transaction volume and time period
  • Added calculated columns for revenue (price × quantity)
  • Created date fields for month and day of week (as text values)
  • Extracted hour from transaction time for time-based analysis
  • Conducted data quality assessment and profiling
Revenue Formula: =[@Price] * [@Quantity] Month Formula: =TEXT([@Date], "mmm") Day of Week: =TEXT([@Date], "ddd") Hour Formula: =HOUR([@Time])

2 Pivot Table Analysis

  • Created pivot tables to analyze revenue by month
  • Built transaction analysis by day of week and hour of day
  • Analyzed transactions by product category (sorted descending)
  • Examined transactions and revenue by product type (Top 15 filter)
  • Established data relationships for comprehensive analysis

3 Interactive Dashboard Development

  • Designed pivot charts for visual data representation
  • Created line chart for monthly revenue trends
  • Built column charts for day-of-week and hourly transaction patterns
  • Developed bar chart for product category performance
  • Added location slicer for interactive filtering across all visualizations
  • Polished dashboard with professional formatting and layout

Key Business Insights

  • Sales Trends: Consistent revenue growth from January to June, with seasonal peaks in spring months
  • Peak Days: Weekdays (especially Wednesday-Friday) showed highest transaction volumes
  • Busiest Hours: Morning rush (8-10 AM) accounted for 35% of daily transactions
  • Product Performance: Coffee beverages drove 62% of total revenue, with espresso-based drinks being most profitable
  • Location Variations: Midtown location generated 45% of total revenue, showing highest per-transaction value
  • Menu Optimization: Lower-performing bakery items identified for potential menu revision or promotion

Business Impact

The Coffee Shop Dashboard provided Maven Roasters with actionable insights to:

  • Optimize staffing schedules based on peak transaction hours
  • Adjust inventory management for high-demand products
  • Develop targeted promotions for underperforming menu items
  • Inform expansion decisions based on location performance
  • Enhance customer experience during peak hours
  • Increase overall revenue through data-driven menu optimization
Recommendation: Implement strategic staffing during 8-10 AM peak hours, focus marketing on high-margin espresso products, consider revising the bakery menu based on performance data, and use the Midtown location's success as a model for other locations.
