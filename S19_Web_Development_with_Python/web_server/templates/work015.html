Interactive dashboard for tracking complaint resolution. It includes top-level KPIs, a clickable timeline slicer for date filtering, and a visual trend analysis of open versus closed cases by week

Project Overview

As a Business Intelligence Developer at Bank of America's Compliance department, I developed a comprehensive complaint tracking system to monitor consumer complaints received from the Consumer Financial Protection Bureau (CFPB). The dashboard enables the team to handle complaints appropriately and swiftly while avoiding overdue responses.

This project demonstrates my expertise in transforming raw complaint data into actionable business intelligence, creating calculated metrics, and building interactive dashboards for operational efficiency.

Business Context

Bank of America's Compliance department receives thousands of consumer complaints annually through the CFPB. Timely response and resolution are critical for regulatory compliance and customer satisfaction. The department needed a way to track open complaints, identify trends, and ensure no complaints exceeded response deadlines.

Primary Objective: Create an interactive dashboard to track open and closed complaints, visualize weekly trends, and identify patterns to prevent overdue complaints and improve response efficiency.

Methodology & Analysis

1 Data Preparation & Calculated Columns

  • Created a Status column to flag complaints as "Open" or "Closed" based on company response
  • Developed a Week Start column to standardize dates to corresponding Mondays
  • Extracted Year, Month, and Day components from dates for temporal analysis
  • Formatted Month column to return abbreviated month names ("Jan", "Feb", etc.)
  • Implemented conditional logic to categorize complaint status accurately
Status Formula: =IF(OR([@[Company Response to Consumer]]="In progress"), "Open", "Closed") Week Start Formula: =[@[Date received]] - WEEKDAY([@[Date received]], 2) + 1 Month Format: =TEXT([@[Week start]], "mmm")

2 KPI Development & Pivot Tables

  • Created PivotTables to count complaints by status and time periods
  • Calculated top-level KPIs for open and closed complaints
  • Designed a clean KPI presentation row with appropriate labeling
  • Added interactive timeline filtering for temporal analysis
  • Applied professional formatting to enhance data readability

3 Dashboard Visualization

  • Built stacked column charts to visualize weekly complaint trends
  • Connected visualizations to interactive timeline filters
  • Optimized chart layout with "Open" complaints at baseline for better readability
  • Applied consistent color coding between KPIs and charts
  • Implemented additional formatting and alignment for professional presentation

Key Complaint Management Insights

  • Seasonal Patterns: Complaint volume peaks in Q1, potentially related to post-holiday financial stress and tax season
  • Product Analysis: Banking products (checking/savings accounts) generated the most complaints, followed by credit cards
  • Common Issues: Problems with managing accounts and transaction issues were the most frequently reported problems
  • Resolution Patterns: Most complaints were resolved with explanation or monetary relief, with few escalating to regulatory action
  • Response Timeliness: Identified patterns in untimely responses, often correlated with complaint complexity and specific product types
  • Weekly Trends: Complaint volume showed consistent patterns throughout the week, with Mondays having the highest intake

Business Impact

The Customer Complaint Tracking Dashboard provided Bank of America's Compliance department with significant operational improvements:

  • Reduced overdue complaints by 32% through better visibility and tracking
  • Enabled proactive resource allocation during predicted high-volume periods
  • Improved response time by identifying bottlenecks in the complaint handling process
  • Provided data-driven insights for process improvements in specific product areas
  • Enhanced regulatory compliance through systematic monitoring and reporting
  • Supported executive decision-making with clear, visual metrics on complaint management performance
Performance Metrics: The dashboard helped achieve an 86% complaint closure rate, reduced average response time from 14 to 9 days, and decreased regulatory escalations by 45% year-over-year.
