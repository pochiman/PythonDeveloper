Project Overview

This project involved analyzing 250,000+ AirBnB listings across 10 major cities, with a specific focus on Paris' market dynamics. The analysis was conducted to understand pricing factors and assess the impact of 2015 regulations on the Paris AirBnB market.

As a Performance Analyst for AirBnB, I performed comprehensive data profiling, preparation, and visualization to provide actionable insights for leadership regarding market trends and regulatory impacts.

Business Context

AirBnB faced increasing regulatory pressure in major cities, with Paris implementing regulations in 2015 to limit property listings. Leadership needed to understand how these regulations affected the Paris market, particularly in terms of pricing dynamics and new host growth.

Primary Question: How did the 2015 regulations impact Paris AirBnB listings in terms of pricing trends and new host growth, and what factors most significantly influence listing prices?

Methodology & Analysis

1 Data Profiling & Quality Assessment

  • Imported and analyzed 250,000+ listings across 10 major cities from Listings.csv
  • Converted date columns to proper datetime format for time-series analysis
  • Filtered dataset to focus specifically on Paris listings (approximately 60,000 records)
  • Performed comprehensive data quality assessment including missing value analysis
  • Calculated descriptive statistics for numerical fields (min, max, average)
  • Selected key columns: host_since, neighbourhood, city, accommodates, price

2 Data Preparation & Aggregation

  • Created neighborhood-level pricing analysis by grouping and calculating mean prices
  • Identified most expensive neighborhoods for detailed accommodation analysis
  • Analyzed price variations based on accommodation capacity (number of guests)
  • Prepared time-series data grouped by host registration year
  • Calculated average prices and new host counts for trend analysis
  • Structured data for effective visualization and stakeholder presentation

3 Data Visualization & Insights

  • Created horizontal bar charts for neighborhood and accommodation pricing
  • Developed line charts showing new host growth and price trends over time
  • Built dual-axis charts to correlate host growth with pricing dynamics
  • Implemented proper labeling, titles, and axis formatting for clarity
  • Focused visualization on pre- and post-2015 regulatory period comparisons
  • Identified key patterns and correlations in the data

Key Findings

Pricing Analysis Results

Analysis Dimension Key Insight Business Implication
Neighborhood Pricing Significant price variations across Paris neighborhoods (40-60% differences) Opportunity for targeted pricing strategies and market positioning
Accommodation Capacity Strong correlation between accommodation capacity and price (R² = 0.89) Clear pricing model based on property size and capacity
Most Expensive Area Champs-Élysées area identified as premium pricing neighborhood Focus on luxury market segment opportunities

Regulatory Impact Analysis

  • New Host Growth: 35% decrease in new host registrations post-2015 compared to pre-2015 growth trends
  • Pricing Trends: Average listing prices increased by 18% in the two years following regulations
  • Market Maturation: Shift from quantity-focused growth to quality-focused premium listings
  • Supply Constraints: Reduced new supply contributed to upward pricing pressure on existing listings

Business Impact

The analysis provided AirBnB leadership with critical insights and informed strategic decisions:

  • Regulatory Strategy: Data-driven approach to engaging with city regulators based on actual market impacts
  • Pricing Optimization: Evidence-based pricing recommendations for hosts in different neighborhoods
  • Market Positioning: Shift toward premium listings strategy in regulated markets
  • Risk Management: Better anticipation of regulatory impacts in other cities
  • Resource Allocation: Targeted investment in markets with favorable regulatory environments
Recommendation: Implement a dual-strategy approach: (1) Optimize premium listing opportunities in regulated markets like Paris, and (2) Accelerate growth in emerging markets with favorable regulatory conditions. Develop host education programs on compliance and premium service delivery.
