002 : AirBnB Listing Analysis

A comprehensive analysis of Paris AirBnB listings to identify pricing factors and regulatory impacts

New AirBnB Hosts in Paris Over Time

2015 Regulations Lead to Fewer New Hosts, Higher Prices

Project Overview

This project involved analyzing Paris AirBnB listings data to identify key factors affecting pricing and evaluate the impact of 2015 regulations on the Paris market. The analysis included data profiling, cleaning, transformation, and visualization to provide actionable insights for business decision-making.

The project demonstrates my expertise in data analysis, quality assessment, and visualization techniques using Python and Pandas to transform raw data into meaningful business intelligence.

Business Context

As AirBnB has grown in popularity, it has increasingly become the focus of regulations designed to limit the number of properties listed in each city. This analysis focused on Paris listings, with particular attention to pricing factors and whether regulations adopted in 2015 impacted listings in the Paris market.

Primary Question: How did the 2015 regulations affect new host registrations and pricing in the Paris AirBnB market?

Methodology & Analysis

1 Data Profiling & Quality Assurance

  • Imported and analyzed 250,000+ listings across 10 major cities
  • Filtered data to focus specifically on Paris listings
  • Performed comprehensive data quality assessment including missing value analysis
  • Calculated minimum, maximum, and average values for all numeric fields
  • Transformed date columns to proper datetime format for time series analysis

2 Data Preparation & Transformation

  • Created neighborhood-level aggregation to identify pricing patterns
  • Analyzed relationship between accommodation capacity and pricing
  • Prepared time-series data to track new host registrations and price changes
  • Filtered to most expensive neighborhoods for detailed analysis
  • Structured data for effective visualization

3 Visualization & Insight Generation

  • Created horizontal bar charts comparing prices across neighborhoods
  • Developed visualizations showing price by accommodation capacity
  • Built time series charts tracking new hosts and average prices over time
  • Implemented dual-axis charts to correlate regulatory impact with market changes
  • Generated actionable insights for business strategy

Key Findings

  • Regulatory Impact: The 2015 regulations correlated with a significant decrease in new host registrations while average prices increased
  • Neighborhood Variation: Discovered substantial price disparities between Paris neighborhoods, with some areas averaging 2-3x higher prices
  • Accommodation Capacity: Identied a non-linear relationship between accommodation capacity and price per night
  • Seasonal Patterns: Detected clear seasonal trends in both pricing and booking activity
  • Data Quality Issues: Uncovered and addressed several data quality issues including missing values and inconsistent formatting

Analysis Techniques

  • Time series analysis

  • Aggregation and summary statistics
  • Data visualization for business intelligence

Business Impact

The analysis provided AirBnB leadership with data-driven insights into:

  • How regulatory changes affected their Paris market operations
  • Pricing strategies based on neighborhood and property characteristics
  • Opportunities for market optimization and growth
  • Data quality improvements needed for more accurate analysis
Recommendation: Implement targeted host acquisition strategies in neighborhoods with optimal price-to-demand ratios while improving data collection processes for more accurate future analysis.
