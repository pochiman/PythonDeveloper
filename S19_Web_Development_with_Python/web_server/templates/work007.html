007 : Global Video Game Sales Analysis

Interactive dashboard analyzing 64,016 video game titles across global markets from 1971-2024

Global video game sales trend from 1971-2024 showing industry growth patterns

Interactive dashboard showing top-selling titles with regional sales breakdown

Project Overview

This project involved analyzing global video game sales data for 64,016 titles released between 1971-2024. The goal was to create an interactive dashboard that provides insights into sales trends, popular genres, top-performing consoles, and regional market differences to come up with strategic decision-making for game development and marketing.

The project demonstrates advanced data profiling, preparation, and visualization skills, culminating in a dynamic tool for executive decision-making.

Business Context

Mavendo Games needed better visibility into the global gaming market to identify promising opportunities, understand regional preferences, and make data-driven decisions about game development, publishing, and market expansion strategies.

Primary Question: How can we transform raw video game sales data into actionable insights that help identify successful patterns across genres, regions, and time periods?

Methodology & Analysis

1 Data Profiling & Quality Assurance

  • Imported and analyzed 64,016 video game titles with sales data from 1971-2024
  • Verified data types and corrected datetime formatting for release dates
  • Standardized column names for consistency (Title, Console, Genre, Publisher, Developer)
  • Created a 'release_year' feature for temporal analysis
  • Performed comprehensive data quality assessment across all regions
  • Identified and handled missing or inconsistent data points

2 Data Preparation & Aggregation

  • Created annual_sales table aggregating total sales by year
  • Generated top10_titles table ranking games by total sales
  • Prepared data for regional analysis (NA, Japan, EU, Other regions)
  • Structured data for genre-based and platform-based analysis
  • Created aggregated views for publisher and developer performance
  • Prepared datasets for interactive visualization components

3 Interactive Dashboard Development

  • Built line chart visualizing sales trends from 1971-2024
  • Created bar chart showing top 10 best-selling games of all time
  • Developed dropdown filters for Title, Genre, Publisher, Developer, and Console
  • Implemented radio button selection for sales metrics (total, NA, Japan, EU, other)
  • Designed responsive layout for optimal viewing experience
  • Ensured real-time updates across all visualization components

Key Findings

Industry Trends & Patterns

Insight Category Key Finding Business Implication
Historical Growth Video game industry shows consistent growth with major spikes during console generation launches Time investments with hardware cycles for maximum impact
Regional Preferences Significant variations in genre popularity across different geographic markets Develop region-specific marketing and content strategies
Genre Specialization Certain consoles demonstrate specialization in specific game genres Align development focus with platform strengths
Cross-regional Hits Identification of titles successful globally vs. region-specific hits Optimize localization and global release strategies

Strategic Insights

  • Top Performing Titles: Wii Sports, Grand Theft Auto V, and Super Mario Bros. dominate all-time sales charts
  • Growth Periods: Major industry growth observed during 1980s (NES era) and 2000s (PS2/Xbox generation)
  • Regional Variations: Japanese market shows strong preference for RPGs while North America favors action/shooter genres
  • Console Specialization: Nintendo platforms excel with family-friendly and platformer games
  • Market Evolution: Shift from arcade-focused to home console to mobile gaming visible in sales patterns

Business Impact

The interactive dashboard and analysis provided Mavendo Games with:

  • Data-driven insights for strategic planning and resource allocation.
  • Identification of promising market segments and genre opportunities.
  • Understanding of regional preferences for targeted game development.
  • Historical context for industry trends and cyclical patterns.
  • Competitive intelligence on successful titles and publishers.
Recommendation: Focus development on action and sports genres for multi-platform release, with special attention to regional preferences. Consider mobile platform expansion given its growing market share, and develop region-specific marketing strategies based on the identified preferences in NA, Japanese, and European markets.
